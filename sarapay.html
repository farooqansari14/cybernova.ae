Customer chooses to pay with either cryptocurrency or traditional fiat currency.

2. Secure Processing

Payment is processed through secure channels with instant verification and confirmation.

3. Blockchain Recording

Transaction details are recorded on blockchain, creating an immutable, transparent record.

4. Settlement & Compliance

Funds are settled to merchant account with automated compliance reporting.
