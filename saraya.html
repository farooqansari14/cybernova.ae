How Saraya Works

Blockchain as the New Land Ledger

Each property on Saraya is linked to a digital token representing its verified ownership record. Governments, developers, and owners can record transfers, mortgages, and other transactions immutably, with timestamped validation and complete auditability.

Core Features

Tamper Proof Title Deeds

Every ownership entry is recorded immutably and verifiably on the blockchain.

Multi Stakeholder Access

Land departments, banks, notaries, and buyers all connected to one system.

Smart Contract Based Transfers

Reduce fraud and remove middlemen with programmable ownership changes.

Geo Tagging Support

Link land parcels to government maps, zoning laws, and project metadata.
