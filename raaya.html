How Raaya Works

Blockchain Meets Medical Infrastructure

Raaya links every patient's medical record to a decentralized health ID. Data from multiple providers is stored immutably and accessed only through patient-approved permissions. Every transaction is logged, traceable, and secure — ensuring trust from first check-up to emergency surgery.
